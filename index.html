My Preferences

Reading, Painting, Crocheting, Badminton

Reading is a sanctuary for my mind, allowing me to escape into different worlds and perspectives. Each book opens a door to new ideas, enriching my understanding of life and humanity. The kinds of books that I like most are detective, science fiction, and novels. Books really fuel my curiosity, inspire my creativity, and help me relax and escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Painting, for me, is a vibrant expression of emotion and thought. With each brushstroke, I explore my inner landscape, translating feelings into colors and forms. This creative process not only relaxes me but also serves as a powerful medium for self-discovery and communication, connecting me to others through shared experiences and visual storytelling.

Crocheting is a meditative practice that connects creativity with craftsmanship. As I weave yarn into intricate patterns, I find a sense of peace and accomplishment. Each project, whether small stuffed animals or a bag, reflects my patience and dedication, reminding me that art can be both functional and beautiful.

Playing badminton adds a dynamic rhythm to my life. Badminton is the greatest way to strengthen my bonds with my friends and family. This sport not only challenges my physical abilities but also sharpens my strategic thinking. The fast-paced nature of the game keeps me energized and focused. Through badminton, I cultivate resilience and adaptability, essential traits in both sport and life.
